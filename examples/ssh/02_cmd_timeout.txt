# SSH 命令超时
# 替换 YOUR_HOST / YOUR_PASSWORD 为实际值后运行

# --- 设置命令超时 ---

# 明文模式:
aitk 'SSH_{"host":"YOUR_HOST","port":22,"user":"root","password":"YOUR_PASSWORD","action":"cmd","cmd":"sleep 2","cmd_timeout":"3s"}'
