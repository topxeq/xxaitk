# SSH 删除远程文件/目录
# 替换 YOUR_HOST / YOUR_PASSWORD 为实际值后运行

# --- 删除文件 ---

# 明文模式:
aitk 'SSH_{"host":"YOUR_HOST","port":22,"user":"root","password":"YOUR_PASSWORD","action":"remove","remote_path":"/tmp/unwanted.txt"}'

# --- 递归删除目录 ---

# 明文模式:
aitk 'SSH_{"host":"YOUR_HOST","port":22,"user":"root","password":"YOUR_PASSWORD","action":"remove","remote_path":"/tmp/olddir","recursive":true}'
