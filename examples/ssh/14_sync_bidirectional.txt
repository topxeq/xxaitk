# SSH 双向同步
# 替换 YOUR_HOST / YOUR_PASSWORD 为实际值后运行

# --- 双向同步 (新文件互相同步) ---

# 明文模式:
aitk 'SSH_{"host":"YOUR_HOST","port":22,"user":"root","password":"YOUR_PASSWORD","action":"sync","direction":"bidirectional","local_path":"/tmp/src","remote_path":"/tmp/dst","recursive":true,"conflict":"newer_wins"}'

# 注意: 双向同步必须指定 conflict 策略，不支持 fail_on_conflict
# 可选: newer_wins, local_wins, remote_wins
